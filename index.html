The world is overgrown. After an alien invasion, a grey, creeping contamination has buried the map.

There's only one way to push it back: real effort, in the real world. Every workout you do turns into ground reclaimed. No effort, no progress — and no way to buy your way ahead.

How it works

A loop in two beats.

Move to claim

Head out for a workout. The route you cover becomes territory you pull back from the contamination, tile by tile.

Clear & rebuild

Spend the effort you earned to clear the contamination and grow your tiles back to green — greenhouses, atmosphere domes, decontamination outposts.

Better together

Team up with people who are actually near you. Allies reinforce and support each other's ground — so "come out for a walk" becomes the strongest move in the game.

What makes it different

Earned, not bought.

Your heart rate is the referee

Effort is measured by real cardiovascular work through Apple Watch and HealthKit. Driving, spoofing your location, and standing still don't count.

However you move, it counts

You don't have to be a runner. A walk, a ride, a session at the gym — even indoors, your effort earns. The map is just where it shows up.

Never pay-to-win

Money never buys an advantage. The only way ahead is to move — so the people putting in the work always come out on top.

Positive-only

You can thank, support, and reinforce the people you play with — never grief them. The social layer is encouragement, not toxicity.

Why it exists

Built to make you healthier.

Underneath the map and the contamination, Reverdure has one job: get you moving more, working a little harder, and coming back to it tomorrow. If a choice ever trades your real fitness for engagement, fitness wins.
